Magis Questions & Answers

Below are a few common questions and answers. These are here to provide you quick answers; they are not meant to discourage you from contacting me directly. Feel free to email me with any comments or questions.

How do I comment on a post?

Try this keyboard combination very slowly. [Down Arrow], [Period], [Enter]. Don't over do it. Instagram has limits on how quickly you post comments. Use the [Up Arrow] to mark posts that you like and the [Down Arrow] to comment on posts that you really like.

Commenting still doesn't work, what else could be wrong?

The commenting feature relies on the word "comment" being found on the page. If you are using Instagram in a language other than English try switching to English at the bottom of the page.

How do I enter my license code?

Click the Magis icon, select Options, then License Number. Paste your license number from the email you received. Make sure there are no spaces before or after the code. You will not get a confirmation but you should no longer see the alert asking you to buy.

How do I disable the reminders?

Click the Magis icon, select Options, then Hourly Activty Reminders. Put an 'x' into the field. No reminders will appear.

I'm not seeing reminders, what do I do?

By default, Magis shows a badge on the icon and makes a short sound reminding you to use Social Media twice an hour. If you aren't seeing or hearing reminders, click the Magis icon, select *Options*, then *Hourly Activty Reminders*. Delete anything in the box.
